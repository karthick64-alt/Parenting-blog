Supporting Your Parenting Journey

Hi, I'm Sarah!

Welcome to Bump & Beyond! I'm a mom of two beautiful children and a passionate advocate for supporting young mothers on their parenting journey. After experiencing the challenges and joys of motherhood firsthand, I created this space to share knowledge, build community, and provide resources that I wish I had when I was starting out.

My journey began when I became a first-time mom at 24. Like many young mothers, I felt overwhelmed by the abundance of conflicting advice and the pressure to be perfect. Through trial and error, support from other moms, and continuous learning, I discovered that there's no one "right" way to parent‚Äîonly what works best for you and your family.

Today, I'm here to share what I've learned, connect you with other amazing parents, and help you feel confident and supported every step of the way.
